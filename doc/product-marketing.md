# Toastit – Product Marketing Brief

## Tagline
**Toast it. Get it done**

From Post-it ideas to Toasted actions.

---

## One-Line Product Description
Toastit is the collaborative workspace that merges todo lists, meeting agendas, note-taking, and follow-up action points into smart, shareable **Toasts**.

---

## Full Product Description
Toastit transforms the simple idea of a sticky note into a powerful, living collaboration tool.

Instead of static post-its that stay pinned to your screen or get lost across multiple apps, every item in Toastit is a **Toast** — a dynamic, contextual object that evolves from raw idea → meeting agenda → assigned tasks → completed action.

Workspaces are intelligently organized around how people actually work:
- **Solo** workspaces for individual projects and personal organization
- **Duo** workspaces for 1:1 meetings and peer collaborations
- **Group** workspaces for team meetings, recurring syncs, workgroups, or ad-hoc sessions

By combining notes, agendas, todos, and follow-ups in one unified flow, Toastit eliminates tool-switching and ensures nothing falls through the cracks.

---

## Core Concept & Naming Story
The name **Toastit** comes from a simple but powerful evolution:

> “It’s the post-it (reminder) — but instead of remaining pinned uselessly on a screen, it gets **shared, discussed, and toasted** into real plans and completed actions.”

A Toast is more than a note.  
A Toast is an idea that has been **toasted** — turned from concept into accountable outcome.

---

## Key Features & Capabilities

### Workspaces
- **Solo** – Private spaces for personal projects, goals, and daily tasks
- **Duo** – Dedicated 1:1 workspaces (manager-direct report, peer coaching, client calls, etc.)
- **Group** – Flexible spaces for recurring team meetings, project workgroups, or spontaneous collaborations

### Toasts – The Core Unit
- Combine notes, agendas, tasks, and action points in a single intelligent item
- Fluid transition from brainstorming → agenda → discussion → follow-up
- Real-time collaboration and commenting
- Built-in owners, deadlines, and status tracking
- Automatic follow-up reminders

### Smart Organization
- Contextual workspaces keep every Toast with the right people and purpose
- No more hunting through scattered meeting notes, Slack threads, or separate task managers
- Seamless flow between personal work and team collaboration

### Collaboration & Accountability
- Instant sharing by inviting people to a workspace
- Clear ownership and due dates on every action point
- Visible progress so everyone knows what’s toasted and what’s still pending
- Meetings end with clear outcomes instead of vague “I’ll follow up” commitments

---

## Target Use Cases
- Individual productivity & personal project management
- Weekly 1:1 meetings between managers and team members
- Recurring team syncs and stand-ups
- Ad-hoc project meetings and brainstorms
- Cross-functional workgroups
- Client or external stakeholder collaborations

---

## Key Benefits
- **Eliminates tool fragmentation** – One app replaces scattered notes, task lists, meeting docs, and follow-up emails
- **Drives real accountability** – Every Toast naturally leads to owners, deadlines, and completion
- **Saves significant time** – No more searching “where did we decide that?”
- **Improves meeting effectiveness** – Agendas and action points stay connected in the same context
- **Feels lightweight but delivers enterprise-grade results** – Simple as a post-it, powerful as a full collaboration OS
- **Reduces meeting fatigue** – Shorter, clearer meetings that actually produce results

---

## Positioning Statement
Toastit is the modern evolution of the sticky note — designed for teams and individuals who want to move beyond passive note-taking and into shared, accountable action.

It sits at the sweet spot between simple note apps (like Apple Notes or Stickies) and heavy project management tools (like Notion, Asana, or Jira), offering just the right amount of structure without the complexity.
